Facts About Hogwarts Founders!!

  • The Names of Hogwarts Founders are: Godric Gryffindor, Helga Hufflepuff, Salazar Slytherin, Rowena Ravenclaw

  • All of the Hogwarts Founders except Slytherin agreed to teach all children with magical parents

  • Each Hogwarts founder choose students based on their personalities and background:

    • Gryffindor chooses students who are brave and chooses to do the right thing-regardless of their lineage

    • Salazar Slytherin chooses only the purebloods

    • Rowena Ravenclaw chooses only the smartest students- regardless of their lineage

    • Helga Hufflepuff chooses the rest who did not fit the other three- regardless of their lineage

  • Salazar Slytherin created the chamber of secrets and speaks parsletongue (language of the snakes!!)

  • Slytherin's Locket, Hufflepuff's Cup and Ravenclaw's Diadem are horcruxes of Lord Voldermort
    (Meaning- Part of his soul is in those items!!)

  • Lord Voldemort (Tom Riddle) is a descendant of Salazar Slytherin
